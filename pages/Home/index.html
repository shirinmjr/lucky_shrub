Your One-Stop Oasis in Tucson, Arizona

At Lucky Shrub, we specialize in garden design, creation, maintenance, and landscaping tailored to the unique Sonoran Desert environment. Our small plant nursery offers a hand-selected variety of indoor plants, succulents, cacti, and drought-tolerant outdoor varieties — making us your complete destination for turning any yard, patio, or indoor space into a thriving, low-water oasis.

Founded by husband-and-wife team Jason and Maria, our passion for plants drives everything we do. Jason designs stunning, functional gardens and leads our expert landscapers, while Maria manages our marketing and nursery to bring you the best plants and service.

  • Landscaping & hardscaping
  • Ongoing garden maintenance
  • Drought-tolerant & xeriscape solutions
  • Plant nursery: indoor & outdoor plants
