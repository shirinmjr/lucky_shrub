Who We Are

Based in Tucson, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance, and landscaping. We also run a small plant nursery offering a curated selection of indoor and outdoor plants, making us a true one-stop shop for all your gardening needs.

Our Story

Lucky Shrub was founded by a passionate husband-and-wife team, Jason and Maria, who have shared a lifelong love for plants and the outdoors.

Jason is our talented garden architect. He leads the creative process, designing custom gardens and overseeing our skilled team of landscapers to bring every vision to life with precision and care.

Maria handles all aspects of marketing and manages our plant nursery, ensuring we offer high-quality plants and excellent customer service to help our clients succeed in their gardening journey.

Together, we built Lucky Shrub from a shared dream into a thriving local business dedicated to sustainable, beautiful landscapes that thrive in the unique Arizona environment.
